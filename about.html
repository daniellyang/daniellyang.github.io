About

(Designer origin story)

Hi there!

I’m a multidisciplinary designer in my third year at the University of Waterloo, studying Computer Science and Finance. I've always been a creative at heart, but I didn't discover my passion for product design until attending my first hackathon in university. At that hackathon, I witnessed the beauty of product design; it was the perfect blend of design, tech, business and psychology that I had been searching for and I've been hooked ever since.

Recently, I've been working on my rapid prototyping skills and systems thinking. Outside of work, I love to play badminton, sing karaoke and trade stocks and financial derivatives. If you wanna get to know me better, we can connect on LinkedIn or feel free to email me. I always love chatting about design and meeting new people. If you want to see some more of my art, you can check out my Dribbble.
